Features

Authentication

For the sake of simplicity for now, you can use any existant or inexistant email to register, it doesn't matter because i didn't establish a connection with gmail api. That being said, if you feel more comfortable signing up with whateveryouwant@gmail.com you can do that just to be able to browse the homepage, the sole purpose of this feature is to learn to implement authentication

Get game details

Once you log in or register, you have access to a variety of games and their details, you can get details of a game by clicking on its photo or on the "Learn more" section if it's available, you get to see the storyline of the game, platforms, release date, and some screenshots

Reviews

Let's say you're interested in a game and want to buy it. Or maybe you have played a game and loved it, or hated it, you can go to the specific game and add a your review for people to see, this review will be saved on the game details page, and in your account page.

About GamePulse

GamePulse was inspired by a love for gaming and a desire to create a community where gamers can stay updated with the latest news and share their own reviews, I have always loved the idea and I'm glad that in this project I got to implement a simple version of it, but I will keep refining and maintaining ths project because i enjoyed doing it as my first project ever. This project was developed as a Portfolio Project for ALX.
